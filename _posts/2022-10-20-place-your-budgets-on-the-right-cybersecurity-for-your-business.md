> One area where security can deliver cost-savings to the business is by speeding up [incident response](https://www.tripwire.com/solutions/incident-detection-and-investigation). Though, achieving more efficient incident response may require some strategizing. Recurring incidents like AV detections or firewall intrusion alerts can be expedited by ensuring you have a robust run-book, which helps responders consistently and reliably deal with everyday issues.
