Uninstalling an antivirus or any other program should always be done through[Add/Remove Programs](http://www.microsoft.com/athome/moredone/cleansweep.mspx) or by using a free third party uninstaller like [Revo Uninstaller](http://www.revouninstaller.com/). - **BitDefender Antivirus Removal Tool:**
- Download the [BitDefender Uninstall Tool](http://www.bitdefender.com/uninstall)  
    First right-click on the red BitDefender icon near the system tray choose on Exit.  
    Wait a few seconds. Afterwards double-click on the BitDefender uninstall tool.  
    You will need to reboot your computer for the changes to take effect.
