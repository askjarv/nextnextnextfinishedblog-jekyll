**emailtest.org**

**Welcome to _emailtest.org_ - the easy way to test that your e-mail is working.**

Simply send an e-mail to [test@emailtest.org](mailto:test@emailtest.org) from your e-mail client and you should receive a reply back. If you do then your e-mail has gone from your network, out through the internet to our server, been processed and sent back to you - proving that your e-mail is working!

If you have any queries, then please e-mail [admin@emailtest.org](mailto:admin@emailtest.org).
